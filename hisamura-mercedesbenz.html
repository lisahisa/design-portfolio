Mercedes Benz Classic Center Poster

2021 • Print

The Mercedes Benz Classic Center located in Irvine, CA specializes in the restoration of classic cars. Visitors can bring in their classic cars for maintenance, or find car parts if they are into DIY. This poster advertises the classic center’s expertise and reliability in order to appeal to a larger audience all around the world.

  • Applications used

    Adobe Illustrator,
    Adobe InDesign

  • Date Completed

    Oct. 2021

  • Project Type

    School Project


Goals

• Spread awareness about the Mercedes Benz Classic Center to more people worldwide
• Increase the number of visitors
• Assure authenticity and reliability and convey expertise to add to the confident nature of the brand


Target Audience

• People with high income and an interest in classic cars
• Baby boomers who want to feel nostalgic
• People looking to fix a vehicle that needs restoration or DIYers

Process

Process

1
Research

Research on the target audience indicated attraction toward nostalgia factors. Furthermore, the Mercedes Benz 300SL Roadster is infamous for having a vintage and classy look, but also the speed and power of a modern-day automobile. This juxtaposition between the antique and the contemporary is a unique point that was highlighted in this project.

2
Ideation

After exploring different visual themes, the Art Deco style was chosen for its recognizable impression of luxury, technology, and beauty during the machine age. Once the aesthetic direction and tagline were chosen several sketches were drawn to prepare for digitization.

3
Revisions

The final design depicts a person driving their Roadster away from their busy day in the city, enjoying their independent time cruising through the night lights. Colors and layouts were adjusted to find the design that best highlighted the car's luxurious nature.
