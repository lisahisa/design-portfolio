Hisamura University Catalog

2021 • Print

Hisamura University is a private institute in San Diego, CA founded in 2016. To celebrate their 5-year anniversary, they introduced a brand new mascot named Penny the Platypus. This catalog advertises school merchandise celebrating the birth of this new mascot to increase school spirit and pride within the student body.

  • Applications used

    Adobe Photoshop,
    Adobe InDesign

  • Date Completed

    Nov. 2021

  • Project Type

    School Project


Goals

• Increase student engagement to build a positive school environment
• Spread awareness about the school to increase the number of applicants
• Increase the sales of school merchandise


Target Audience

• The student body who is looking for more school spirit on campus
• Potential college applicants who may want to attend Hisamura University in the future.

Process

Process

1
Research

Research on the target audiences revealed that young adults sought for modern-looking designs with a rather bright, friendly, and experimental aesthetic. This indicated the need to create a design that captured these ideals yet still holistically maintained the school’s visual identity.

2
Ideation

Thumbnail sketches were created to explore various styles and determine a layout for the catalog. Once a visual direction was selected, the sketches were refined into tighter renditions that drafted aesthetic decisions before digitization.

3
Revisions

The catalog design was rendered on Adobe InDesign, with images and copy formatted in an 8-column grid. Revisions were made to finalize a design that achieved the objectives of the project conceptually and aesthetically. Multiple test prints were conducted to verify legibility as a physical booklet.
