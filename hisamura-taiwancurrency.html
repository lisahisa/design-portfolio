Taiwan Currency Redesign

2021 • Print

History repeatedly shows the country of Taiwan as a colonized nation, with the world yet confusing Taiwan as either an independent state or purely an extension of China. This Taiwanese currency redesign targets Taiwanese citizens and residents to gain more pride in their country by celebrating Taiwan's beauty and history as an independent country. The project also targets people abroad, allowing them to see that Taiwan is the center of great technological innovation, as well as of art, nature, and life. Despite its complicated history and continued controversy, Taiwan is independently a country full of character and uniqueness.

  • Applications used

    Adobe Photoshop

  • Date Completed

    Dec. 2021

  • Project Type

    School Project


Goals

• Design currency that encompasses Taiwan's individual beauty
• Encourage citizens to have more pride and dignity toward Taiwan's success as an independent sovereign state
• Improve Taiwan's international recognition to increase relations between Taiwan and other foreign countries


Target Audience

• Taiwanese citizens and residents that view Taiwan as an individual country, independent from China
• People interested in Taiwan’s unique culture and beauty

Process

Process

1
Research

Research on the country of Taiwan was conducted and organized in a mind map, brainstorming parts of Taiwanese culture that could be represented in the currency design.

2
Ideation

Thumbnail sketches were drawn based on research of cultural symbols and motifs of Taiwan. Each currency design explores a distinct theme, with every visual component positioned to follow the golden spiral.

3
Revisions

Currency design was put through a series of edits and revisions in order to determine the best composition for the project. Final design, front and back, was printed in 100% color.
