Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Lisa and I am a college student studying graphic design, aiming to use my creative knowledge to make a positive impact on the world. In my eyes, successful design comes from the integration of thorough research and creative problem-solving. A visual solution doesn’t solely come from a measure of aesthetics—what’s also expected is the designer’s willingness to understand the issue at large and push the project to its full potential. My qualities of curious experimentation, continuous efforts for improvement, and wish for developing everlasting relationships get me far to distribute design solutions that will benefit the greater good.
